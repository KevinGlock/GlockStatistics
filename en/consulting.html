Consulting

Do you need help with the methodical implementation of your research project?

My knowledge includes understanding, interpreting and applying qualitative and quantitative methods of empirical social research as well as mathematical statistics. We go through all the relevant points intensively and in a simple way. The focus is on answering your questions. I would be happy to provide suitable methodological literature and help you formulate research questions, hypotheses and measurement-theoretical aspects. So that you can be sure to carry out your survey and evaluation adequately.

Commonly used program packages are: SPSS, Stata and R. But Excel, Jamovi and PSPP are also possible, if you prefer that.

I can probably help you answer your questions, just contact me (kevin (dot) glock (at) stud (dot) uni (minus) bamberg (dot) de). Then everything can be discussed without obligation.
