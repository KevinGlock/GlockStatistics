Analyses

Do you need help analyzing your data?

My knowledge includes understanding, interpreting and applying qualitative and quantitative methods of empirical social research as well as mathematical statistics. We go through all the relevant points intensively and in a simple way.

The focus is on answering your questions. Depending on your needs,

  • I provide you with suitable literature for your methodology,
  • help you formulate research questions and hypotheses,
  • but also measurement-theoretical aspects (question construction, method selection, sample formulation),
  • create descriptive statistics and
  • perform inferential statistical analysis and hypothesis testing.
  • I also create syntax documents with you or for you,
  • Correlation and independence tests,
  • Reliability and scale analyzes,
  • structure checking and
  • structure-discovering analyzes.

I would also be happy to help you with the structure and formatting of your documents and the presentation of the results, so that you can be sure that you will carry out your analysis adequately and in a manner that is ready for publication and that you can publish it.

Commonly used program packages are: SPSS, Stata and R. But Excel, Jamovi and PSPP are also possible, if you prefer that.
